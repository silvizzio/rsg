The Environment (ENV) view tracks air quality, weather, and the regenerative and conservation metrics that Red Sea Global monitors across the destination. The ENV persona loads on the shared map canvas, with its own left and right panels and a persona-scoped alert tray.

<ScreenshotPlaceholder caption="ENV persona, Shura Island, map LOD 1" />

## What you see

| Field | Example | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Air Quality Index | Good | Current air quality band for the destination |
| Weather | 31C, clear | Live conditions at the selected zone |
| Conservation Status | Stable | Trend for the active conservation zone |
| Environmental Alerts | 1 | Open environmental items |
| Active Layer | Air Quality | The layer currently driving the map overlay |

## Working in the ENV view

1. Open the ENV persona from the persona bar.
2. Read destination-wide environmental bands at map LOD 1.
3. Select a conservation zone to inspect its metrics at map LOD 2.
4. Open an environmental alert to see the affected site at map LOD 3.

<Callout type="note">
Marine and coastal environmental detail sits in the Marine Operations view.
</Callout>
