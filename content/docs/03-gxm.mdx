The Guest Experience Management (GXM) view is the destination-wide executive picture of guest flow, occupancy, and experience health across Red Sea Global. The GXM persona loads on the shared map canvas, with its own left and right panels and a persona-scoped alert tray.

![GXM at map LOD 1, destination overview.](03-gxm-lod1-1.jpg)

## What you see

| Field | Example | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Experience Score | 92 | Composite guest experience index for the destination |
| Occupancy | 78 percent | Current occupancy across active assets |
| Guest Flow | Steady | Movement trend across key zones |
| Experience Alerts | 2 | Open items affecting guest experience |
| Active Layer | Guest Flow | The layer currently driving the map overlay |

## Map LOD 2: the district view

At map LOD 2 the GXM view holds the Marina District, with the guest experience strip running along the bottom.

![GXM at map LOD 2, Marina District. The bottom strip reads 3 of 5 stations over capacity, 42 guests traveling from the Marina, 4.4 of 5 travel satisfaction, transfer variance of +42%, and 6 of 12 vehicles available.](03-gxm-lod2.jpg)

## Map LOD 3: the guest trip lifecycle

At map LOD 3 the GXM view drills into the Marina and walks the guest trip across three phases, each surfacing its own recommended action.

### Pre-trip

![GXM at map LOD 3, Marina, pre-trip phase. The recommended action card, badged ABOVE CAPACITY, calls to request +6 transfer boats from OPS for the Marina, triggered by demand of 214 against the 150 marina capacity with 11 vehicles available and the oldest request at 8 min.](03-gxm-lod3-pre-trip.jpg)

### Trip

![GXM at map LOD 3, Marina, trip phase. The recommended action card, badged WATCH OCCUPANCY, calls to dispatch 2 buggies to the Spine Corridor, triggered by 318 guests traveling with the lead buggy at 5 of 6 seats.](03-gxm-lod3-trip.jpg)

### After-trip

![GXM at map LOD 3, Marina, after-trip phase. The recommended action card, badged OVER TARGET, calls to follow up on a low-rated arrival and review the Marina to villas leg, triggered by satisfaction averaging 4.1 of 5 with one group at 3.6 and travel time of 8.4 min against a 6.3 min target.](03-gxm-lod3-after-trip.jpg)

## Working in the GXM view

1. Open the GXM persona from the persona bar.
2. Read the destination Experience Score at map LOD 1.
3. Select a district to inspect its occupancy and flow at map LOD 2.
4. Open an experience alert to see the affected site at map LOD 3.

<Callout type="note">
GXM is a read and oversight view. Dispatch and incident actions are performed in the OPS view.
</Callout>
