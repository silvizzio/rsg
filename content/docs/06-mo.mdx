The Marine Operations (MO) view covers vessel and marine traffic, dive sites, and coastal and marine conservation zones across the Red Sea destination. The MO persona loads on the shared map canvas, with its own left and right panels and a persona-scoped alert tray.

![MO, map LOD 1](06-mo-lod1-1.jpg)

## What you see

| Field | Example | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Active Vessels | 12 | Tracked vessels in the destination waters |
| Dive Sites | 5 open | Dive sites currently active |
| Marine Alerts | 0 | Open marine and coastal items |
| Map LOD | LOD 1 | Destination overview tier |
| Active Layer | Vessel Traffic | The layer currently driving the map overlay |

## Working in the MO view

1. Open the MO persona from the persona bar.
2. Read vessel and marine traffic across the destination at map LOD 1.
3. Select a marine zone to inspect vessels and dive sites at map LOD 2.
4. Open a vessel or dive site to see its detail at map LOD 3.

<Callout type="note">
Coastal environmental bands shared with the ENV view are read-only here.
</Callout>
