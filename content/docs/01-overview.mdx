The RSG Integrated Operations Centre (IOC) Digital Twin is Vizzio's live 3D operational picture of Red Sea Global destinations. It gives four operator personas (Guest Experience Manager, Operations Manager, Environment Manager, and Marine Operations Manager) a role-specific view of the destination across three map detail levels, all on a single Mapbox GL JS canvas.

![The IOC at startup: Shura Island in the Guest Experience Manager view, map LOD 1.](03-gxm-lod1-1.jpg)

## What the IOC does

The IOC renders Red Sea Global destinations as an interactive 3D twin. Operators switch between personas to load a view tuned to their role, drill from a destination-wide overview down to a single site, watch live closed-circuit television (CCTV) feeds, respond to alerts as they surface, and replay scripted scenarios in simulation. The map is the shared surface: every persona, panel, and alert resolves to a place on it.

## Personas

The IOC ships with four operator personas. Confirm the exact expansions against the RSG IOC Technical statement of work.

| Persona | Expansion | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Guest Experience Manager | Guest Experience Management | Destination-wide executive view, guest flow, experience KPIs and alerts |
| Operations Manager | Operations | Live incident management, CCTV grid, dispatch, operational alerts |
| Environment Manager | Environment | Air quality, weather, regenerative and conservation metrics |
| Marine Operations Manager | Marine Operations | Vessel and marine traffic, dive sites, coastal and marine alerts |

## Map levels of detail

The IOC uses three map detail levels, written here as map LOD to keep them distinct from the documentation layering pattern. Each level changes what the map and the analysis panel show.

- **Map LOD 1, destination overview**: the full destination with aggregate KPIs across all active layers
- **Map LOD 2, district zoom**: a selected district or zone with its metrics, status, and neighbouring zones
- **Map LOD 3, site detail**: a single site or asset with full context, live feeds, and recommended actions
