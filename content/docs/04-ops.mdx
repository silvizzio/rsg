The Operations (OPS) view is the live incident surface where operators monitor alerts, watch the CCTV grid, and dispatch responses across the active sector. The OPS persona loads on the shared map canvas, with its own left and right panels and a persona-scoped alert tray.

<ScreenshotPlaceholder caption="OPS persona, Shura Island, map LOD 1" />

## What you see

| Field | Example | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Active Alerts | 4 | Open incidents in the current sector |
| CCTV Grid | 3x3 | Camera tiles for the selected district |
| Map LOD | LOD 2 | District-level detail tier |
| Simulation Mode | Off | When on, the view replays a scripted scenario |
| Dispatch Status | 1 en route | Responses currently in progress |

## Working in the OPS view

1. Open the OPS persona from the persona bar.
2. Select a sector to load its alerts and CCTV grid.
3. Acknowledge an alert to move it from Open to Acknowledged.
4. Dispatch a response and confirm the alert advances to In Progress.

<Callout type="note">
Alert states and the full incident lifecycle are documented in Alerts and Incidents.
</Callout>
